CIS Wuxi

College of International Students-CIS is called the International Education faculty in NUIST. It is stablished in 2008. Now NUIST Wuxi campus has CIS faculty as well. CIS offering three English medium bachelor's degree programme with Excellent Freshermen Scholarship. Each major has significant amount of students quota for getting admission.

CIS Depertments and Majors

Computer Science and Technology

Generally, computer science refers to designing and building computers and computer programs also information technology. The program of Computer Science and Technology provides intensive preparation in the concepts and techniques related to the design, programming, researching and application of computing systems. Students are provided a deep understanding of both fundamentals and important current issues in computer science and computer engineering so that they may either obtain productive employment or pursue advanced degrees.

Electronic Information Engineering

Electronic Information Engineering also called Electronics and Communications Engineering. Electronics covers subfields such as analog electronics, digital electronics, embedded systems and microprocessor. Electronics engineering deals with implementation of applications, principles and algorithms developed within many related fields, for example solid-state physics, radio engineering, telecommunications, control systems, signal processing, computer engineering, instrumentation engineering, robotics, and many others.

International Trade and Economy

CIS Wuxi offering a bachelors degree on International Trade and Economy. It is one of the leargest major of CIS. International trade allows countries to expand their markets and access goods and services that otherwise may not have been available domestically. As a result of international trade, the market is more competitive. This ultimately results in more competitive pricing and brings a cheaper product home to the consumer. International trade was key to the rise of the global economy. In the global economy, supply and demand—and thus prices—both impact and are impacted by global events.
